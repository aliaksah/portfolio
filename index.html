About Me

I am an Associate Professor with a focus on Statistics, Artificial Intelligence, Econometrics, Machine Learning, and Operations Research. My academic journey spans across three prestigious institutions, culminating in a PhD in Statistics from the University of Oslo, Norway.

My research interests include Bayesian statistics, model selection, and probabilistic machine learning. I have published extensively in these areas and am actively involved in mentoring the next generation of scholars.
